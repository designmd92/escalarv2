    Series Presentation

  • Didactic strategy appropriate for each level in elementary education; to assure the comprehension of the subjects with an adequate graphical design that in addition to providing visual agility in said comprehension, adds esthetic value, fresh and relaxed and interesting which is appealing to the student.

  • Texts integrated in the same issue and Social Sciences.

  • The development of every Unit contains an integration focus bringing the student closer to contextual situations from his own experience or from his previous knowledge.

  • Activities from diverse natural observation, comprehension, reflection, value, creation, application and investigation are efficiently alternated through the teacher’s act.

  • Recreates and prepares spaces for explanations, examples, questions, homework proposals that motivate the active participation of the students.

  • In the teaching-learning process, individual attention is favored to the students, which will be complemented with reinforcement activities with the aim of recuperating those basic contents and objectives with amplified activities whenever possible.

  • With Universal Standards in Science and adjusted to the requirements and rigor of the established parameters by the Ministry of Education in Colombia.
